Born from necessity.

Watching movies on a MacBook is an incredible visual experience, but it's often ruined by black bars and a persistent cursor.

"Why isn't there a native switch for this?"

We looked at existing solutions. They were bulky, built on Electron (slow), or tracked user data. We wanted something that felt like it came pre-installed on macOS.

So we built CursorFixerS. Pure Swift. Zero bloat.
