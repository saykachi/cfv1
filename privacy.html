Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 4, 2025

1. Introduction

MiraiLab ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension, Cursor Fixer (the "Extension"), collects, uses, and protects your information.

The Extension is designed to operate locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, browsing history, or video content to any external servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We believe in data minimization. The Extension functions entirely within your browser environment.

Personal Information:

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.

Browsing History:

We do not track, record, or transmit the websites you visit. The Extension analyzes web pages solely in real-time to detect HTML5 video elements.

Video Content:

The Extension interacts with video players to provide features such as Speed Control, Zoom, Picture-in-Picture, and Ad Skipping. This interaction happens locally via the Document Object Model (DOM). We do not know what videos you are watching.

Local Preferences:

User settings (e.g., hotkeys, auto-skip status, theme preferences) are stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API. This data is never synchronized with our servers.

3. Permissions Explained

To function correctly, Cursor Fixer requires specific permissions. Here is a transparent explanation of why each permission is needed:

"Read and change all your data on the websites you visit" (<all_urls> / activeTab):

This permission is technically required for the Extension to detect video players on any website (e.g., YouTube, Netflix, Twitch, and others) and inject the control interface (Skip button, HUD).

  • How we use it: We scan the page structure (DOM) to find <video> tags and overlay our controls.
  • What we DO NOT do: We do not read your passwords, emails, form inputs, or page text content.

"Storage":

Used to save your personal configuration (e.g., whether "Auto Skip" is enabled or disabled) so that your settings persist between browser sessions.

"Scripting":

Used to execute the code responsible for video acceleration and ad detection within the current tab.

4. Ad Blocking and Skipping Features

The "Auto Skip" and "Ad Blocker" features work by analyzing the HTML structure of the video player to detect technical indicators of advertisements (such as specific class names or video duration). This process is automated and occurs locally. We do not track which ads are displayed or skipped.

5. Screenshot Feature

When you use the "Snapshot" feature, the Extension captures a single frame from the video element using the HTML5 Canvas API. This image is generated locally in your browser's memory and is immediately prompted for download to your device. We do not have access to these images, and they are never uploaded to any server.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) or advertising networks. No data is shared with third parties.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
