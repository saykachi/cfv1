Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. CursorFixerS operates entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit any personal data.

No Personal Data

We do not ask for your name, email, or IP address.

Local Processing

All functionality and settings are handled on your Mac.

Effective Date: November 25, 2025 • Version 1.0

1. Core Principle and Scope

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of MiraiLab ("we," "us," or "our") concerning the CursorFixerS macOS Safari Extension (the "Service"). This policy is built on the foundation of **Privacy by Design**. We only process the absolute minimum data required to execute the user's requested action (e.g., changing video speed).

Crucially: The Service operates without a backend server connection for user data collection.

2. Data We Explicitly Do NOT Collect

Because the Service operates locally, we do not have access to, nor do we collect, any of the following categories of information:

  • Identifiers: Real name, email address, IP address, device ID, unique user ID, or network data.
  • Sensitive Data: Financial information, health data, or precise geolocation.
  • User Activity Data: Browsing history, search queries, click paths, or video watch history.
  • Diagnostics/Analytics: No crash reports, performance logs, or usage statistics are sent to MiraiLab.

If an update introduces optional, anonymized analytics (e.g., via Apple's built-in system), this policy will be updated with explicit opt-out instructions. As of Version 1.0, no such data is collected.

3. Technical Access and Permissions

The Service requires permission to **"Read and alter webpages"** to fulfill user commands. This is a technical requirement of Safari Extensions, and its purpose is limited to the following technical actions:

Video Manipulation

Accessing the HTML5 `

Cursor Hiding

Implementing the Ghost Cursor feature requires injecting temporary CSS (`cursor: none;`) when the mouse stops moving. We only monitor mouse input within the current Safari window to trigger this change.

4. Data Storage and Retention

4.1 Local Storage

The Service uses **browser local storage** (`localStorage` or equivalent APIs) to save non-personal user preferences, such as:

  • The last set playback speed (e.g., 1.5x).
  • The default state for the PiP feature.
  • Toggle state for the Ghost Cursor feature.

This data is physically stored on your Mac and is never transmitted to MiraiLab or any third parties. Deleting the App or clearing your Safari's browsing data will typically remove this local storage.

4.2 Data Retention

Since MiraiLab does not collect any data, there is no personal data to retain, store securely, or destroy.

5. Third Parties and Children's Privacy

5.1 Third-Party Websites

CursorFixerS interacts with third-party websites (YouTube, Netflix, etc.). This Privacy Policy only governs the use of the CursorFixerS extension. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of the sites they visit.

5.2 Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly collect personal data from Children. If you, as a parent or guardian, become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can address the situation.

6.1 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, but due to the non-collection of contact information, we cannot notify users directly. We advise reviewing this document periodically.
